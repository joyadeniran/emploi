## The service

Emploi generates tailored job-application documents (cover letters and CVs)
from your real experience, and screens employers for common scam signals. It
is a tool that assists your job search; it does not apply on your behalf,
guarantee interviews, or guarantee that any employer is legitimate.

## Honest applications

Emploi is built to never fabricate experience: generated documents draw only
on what your CV and profile actually say, and anything stretched is marked
"(stretch — verify)". You are responsible for reviewing every document before
sending it and for the truthfulness of anything you submit to an employer.

## Employer verification is risk reduction, not a guarantee

Trust scores are computed from public technical evidence (domain records,
website checks, scam-pattern language). A high score does not certify an
employer, and a scammer with a real website can still pass. Regardless of any
score: never pay a fee to apply or to be hired, and never share bank details,
BVN, NIN, or identity documents during an application process.
