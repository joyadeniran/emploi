Scam protection

Protection you can actually see

Every trust score is computed from real, named evidence — never from an AI's opinion. Here's a pattern Emploi catches:

The "registration fee" scam, caught in seconds

Fake employers flood WhatsApp groups, Telegram channels and job sheets everywhere — charging fees, harvesting ID and bank details, promising salaries that never come.

Before you apply, Emploi checks:

  • Employer domain & mail-server records
  • Live website and company-name consistency
  • The posting itself against known scam patterns
  • A shared blacklist that compounds over time
